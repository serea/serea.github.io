## Abstract

This paper proposes a novel approach based on network embedding to identify lateral movement attacks. The method converts network traffic data into low-dimensional vector representations, leveraging network embedding to capture inherent relationships, thereby effectively identifying patterns and trajectories of lateral movement attacks.
